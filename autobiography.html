My Autobiography

About Me

Janrick Albert P. Sentes is a young man with a clear vision for his future and the determination to make it happen. He is currently a student at SCCI, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Criminology, a field that aligns with his deep interest in justice, order, and public safety. Through his studies, Janrick aspires to one day contribute to society as a dedicated professional in law enforcement. Outside the classroom, Janrick finds strength and discipline in the sport of boxing. For him, boxing is more than just a pastime; it is a way of life. It teaches him resilience, focus, and the importance of pushing through challenges. Every time he steps into the ring, he feels a sense of purpose, knowing that the values he learns in boxing apply not only in sports but also in life. When he isn’t studying or training, Janrick enjoys unwinding by watching movies. Films serve as his escape, allowing him to explore different worlds, experience new perspectives, and recharge his mind. Whether it’s an action-packed story or a thought-provoking drama, movies offer him both entertainment and inspiration. Janrick’s journey at SCCI is one of growth and hard work. Balancing his passion for boxing, his love of movies, and the demands of his studies has taught him valuable lessons about perseverance and time management. He is dedicated to making the most of every opportunity, preparing himself for a future where he can make a meaningful impact in his community. Through his unwavering dedication, Janrick Albert P. Sentes exemplifies the spirit of a fighter—someone who faces challenges head-on, stays true to his passions, and continually strives to achieve his goals.
